Treeman 19600

Team 19600 is a high-school robotics team from YK Pao School, Shanghai. We give students opportunities to experience real world engineering through competitive robotics.

YK Pao School

Visit Site

Team 19600 consists of students from YK Pao School in Shanghai, China. The team was founded in 2018 and has grown to become one of the most successful FTC teams in the region.

FIRST Robotics

Visit Site

FIRST is an international organization which uses competitive robotics as a vehicle for promoting science & technology. FIRST allows high school students to work side by side with professional mentors to learn skills ranging from engineering to marketing, animation and business.
